The Marathon Clothing Store

The Marathon Clothing Store was inaugurated by Nipsey along with partners Carless, the head of the agency, Karen Civil and his brother Samiel Asghedom in 2017, and started a co-working environment which he named "Vector 90". The Marathon Clothing Store was named by Nipsey. The name itself was, because of the Nipseys early struggle in life as a member of a gang called Crips and his strive for the greatness. Nipsey was always trying to become a better man and the best version of himself, so he considered he was always "running a marathon" - run through ups and downs,through hard and happy times, he had big goals in life and great vision, so he was running always to that goal. That's why, for him, the marathon continues always and that's why he gave the store name "The Marathon Clothing Store".

The Purpose of The Store

The Marathon Clothing Store is on the corner of two the most violent streets in south Los Angeles called Slauson and Crenshaw. Crenshaw was the place where Nipsey grew up and raised as a Crip. It was a poor, violent street full of drug dealers and crime. Nipsey was trying to put both of the streets on the map, because for him, those streets were beautiful places even when he knew what was going on. The purpose of the store was to bring people in those streets together, to promote Crenshaw - his neighborhood and all the poor people in there. As The Marathon Clothing Store was building and growing, so were Crenshaw and Slauson. People started coming by, visiting the store, buying shirts, hats, sweaters and representing Crenshaw and Slauson. That way, Nipsey and his store put Crenshaw and Slauson on map and gave better reputation of that hood. After Nipseys death, Los Angeles City Councilmember Marqueece Harris-Dawson announced that the intersection of Crenshaw and Slauson will become Ermias "Nipsey Hussle" Asghedom Square, because of what he had done for those quarts.
